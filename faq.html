How often should I cut my hair?

The frequency of hair undercutting depends on their initial length. We cut short hair every 4-6 weeks (because a short haircut quickly loses its shape and shape), while hair is long every 6-8 weeks. Recessing results from the need to refresh the tips - exposed to mechanical damage and sensitized by the action of external factors. Properly adjustable tips prevent further splitting.

Can the hair get used to the product?

Hair needs proper care and hydration. When we use a long time the same shampoo or conditioner, we can get the impression that these products do not work on our hair anymore. This may be due to excess protein in the product. Our hair does not need such a complex product anymore, it can be too heavy. It is worth changing the conditioner / shampoo for cosmetics from the basic care line. Hair does not "get used to" the product, it can simply be badly chosen. Most hair cosmetics available in supermarkets and small shops contain silicone, which coats the hair with a layer that prevents the nutrients. The result is dull hair and difficult to shape. At the beginning of the use of silicone products, the hair is more shiny and moisturized, but it is a short-lived condition. To rinse the silicone layer it is necessary to use products containing strong detergents. Professional cosmetics, if they contain silicone, it is a silicone of a different kind, so-called "Good" - it dissolves in water and does not affect the hair in a harmful way.

I often dye my hair, but the color quickly loses its intensity. What can I do to make it last longer?

The rate of loss of color intensity depends to a large extent on the structure of the hair. Sensitive hair, the scales of which do not close in a shorter time lose their color depth. In order to enjoy the color for a longer time, we provide proper care for the hair. Cosmetics dedicated to dyed hair, containing acidic pH are necessary. This property helps smooth the hair and gives it a shine.

How can I get back to my natural color after long-lasting coloring?

If the hair is dyed in a dark color and the natural color is lighter - we can return to it by decolourisation. In the opposite situation, when we return from darkness to dark, we perform pigmentation.

I've always dreamed about wavy hair. How can I get natural waves?

If the hair has a tendency to slightly wave, we can use their structure using appropriate hair cutting techniques. Specially selected cut will mobilize hair to wave. The next step is stylization using a diffuser, curling iron or straightener (the method depends on the type of hair and the type of waves we want to achieve).

How to choose the right products for hair care and styling at home?

The right products should be selected for the type of hair and the condition of the scalp. It is best to use professional cosmetics, containing selected, high-quality ingredients that do not cause "side effects". Choosing cosmetics, let's consult our hairdresser.
